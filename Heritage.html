Hello!

I am Heritage Osineye Adesola.


I recently graduated from Ben Davis University High school with an Academic & Technical Honors Diploma, and an Associate Degree in Science.


I was involved in National Honor Society and the International Key Club. I was the co-manager of my school's chapter of The Habitat for Humanity Project & The African Student Association.


I received a full-ride scholarship to IUPUI. I plan to major in Nursing, and minor in Africana Studies & Informatics. My hobbies include trying new things, going out with my friends, practicing my cosmetology skills, and watching TV.


I am a Congress of Future Medical Leaders Award of Excellence nominee. I was elected Class Secretary. I served as a member of the Key Club and the manager of the Habitat for Humanity Project. I currently take classes to be a certified Medical Interpreter.


I am an AVID student. I have been a school Ambassador. I also volunteer with the Red Cross. I participated in Cross-Country, Academic SuperBowl, Book Club and I started my own business, “Malaika Beaute” while in high school.
